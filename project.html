Community Development Report
Nitte Grampanchayat, Karnataka, India
Prepared: February 2026

Executive Summary
This report consolidates demographic data, facilities, resources,
and government schemes for Nitte Grampanchayat, Karnataka.
It aims to support transparent planning based on real community needs.


1. Demographic Profile
Population: ~6,000-7,000 (Census 2011 + growth estimates)
Households: ~1,200
Literacy Rate: ~85%
Age Distribution: Youth-heavy, with significant elderly population

Economy:
Agriculture: paddy, arecanut, coconut

3. Government Schemes
Government Schemes (Expanded - 50+ Schemes)

Rural Development & Employment

1. MGNREGA - Guarantees 100 days of wage employment for rural households. Apply via Panchayat office with a job card.
2. DDU-GKY - Skill training for rural youth (18–35 years). Apply through Rural Skill Centres.
3. PMKVY - National skill certification program. Free training in trades.
4. SVEP - Supports rural entrepreneurship with loans and training.
5. NRLM (Aajeevika) - Promotes Self-Help Groups (SHGs) for women’s livelihood
